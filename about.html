About Skylinee LLC

Skylinee LLC is a trusted IT services provider serving clients throughout the Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia (DMV) area. We specialize in designing and implementing reliable, scalable networking and cybersecurity solutions for both private and public sector organizations.

With years of experience in federal, state, and local government IT projects, our team brings the technical skill and professionalism needed to handle complex environments. From structured cabling and secure firewall deployments to cloud migrations and remote support, Skylinee LLC is equipped to meet your IT infrastructure needs.

Our mission is to empower businesses with seamless, secure, and modern IT systems that support growth and resilience in a connected world.
