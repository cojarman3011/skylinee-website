About us

Our Company

Skylinee LLC is a premier Information Technology services provider based in Baltimore, Maryland, delivering cutting-edge solutions that power modern business operations. With a strong foundation in network infrastructure, cybersecurity, and end-user support, Skylinee has consistently earned the trust of partners across the private and public sectors. We specialize in the delivery, configuration, and setup of critical network systems—including routers, switches, firewalls, servers, and endpoint devices. From local office deployments to large-scale remote rollouts, our team ensures seamless connectivity, security, and performance. Skylinee's expertise includes: Network Engineering: Installing and configuring reliable, scalable infrastructure. Help Desk & NOC Support: Keeping your systems online with fast, professional remote and onsite service. Cybersecurity Operations: Enhancing your protection against evolving digital threats through proactive defense and compliance. Field Services: Trusted by businesses to implement solutions with precision, speed, and accountability. Our technicians have worked side-by-side with Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, ISPs, and managed service providers—earning a reputation for excellence, adaptability, and technical leadership. At Skylinee LLC, we don’t just solve problems—we anticipate them. Our mission is to empower organizations with smart, secure, and sustainable IT solutions that support long-term growth and peace of mind.
